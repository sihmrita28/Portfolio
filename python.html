Quiz Platform

Naan Mudhalvan Project

Home/ Quiz Platform

Objective

The objective of this project is to develop an AI-powered quiz platform that generates interactive and personalized quizzes for high school students. The platform is designed to align with curriculum standards and promote active learning while ensuring academic integrity through intelligent question design and system safeguards.

Methodology

Design Thinking + Agile Approach:
The project adopts a hybrid methodology combining Design Thinking for user-centric innovation and Agile for iterative development and adaptability.

  • User needs are first identified and translated into clear objectives.
  • Quiz content is generated using AI models and refined through prototyping and feedback cycles.
  • Iterative sprints ensure continuous improvement and integration of user feedback.

AI Tools and Automation:
AI models like ChatGPT, Gemini AI, and the Revisely AI Quiz Generator are used to:

  • Automatically create curriculum-aligned quiz questions.
  • Adapt question difficulty based on learning behavior.
  • Generate multiple question types (MCQs, T/F, etc.) for varied engagement.

Visualization / Platform Features:
The platform includes intelligent features such as real-time feedback, adaptive question difficulty, and randomized quiz sequences to maintain academic integrity. It provides detailed insights into student performance and helps identify areas for improvement. The interface is designed to be accessible, intuitive, and scalable, with support for a wide range of subjects including science, mathematics, and literature. These features collectively enhance the platform's ability to provide meaningful and engaging learning experiences.

User Interaction and Experience:
Students interact with the platform through personalized quizzes that are generated based on their progress and response patterns. As students complete quizzes, the system analyzes their performance and adapts future questions accordingly. Educators can use the platform to monitor student progress, identify gaps in understanding, and offer targeted support. Measures such as time limits, plagiarism detection, and randomized question banks ensure that assessments remain fair and secure. Overall, the platform promotes active learning and discourages rote memorization or shortcut approaches.
